Welcome To ZFL SWAP

ZFL SWAP is a decentralized protocol for automated liquidity provision on Ethereum. We Provide: SWAP and Decentralised Exchanges, OTC, In House & P2P Loans, Staking and Stablecoins! We have the latest in decentralized exchanges which give you the choice of becoming a maker, taker & becoming a liquidity provider or just swap assets, we shall also provide solutions on how to trade bitcoin in the ETH ecosystem using counter parties.
Our token is ZFLGold, we providing burning system on that with very low supply, also Each ZFLGold token holder has the right to control our stable coins and send proposals to alter the platform rules that will be voted on by the community which is also commonly called a Governance token.
